## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- **Hardware**: Stage 1 (DDR4) or Stage 2 (FPGA Dual Node)
- **Network**: 500Mbps wired connection
- **Power**: UPS with 5G wireless backup
- **Wallet**: USDC (Stage 1) or USDC + USDT (Stage 2)
